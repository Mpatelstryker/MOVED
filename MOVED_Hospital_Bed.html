The Future of Hospital Mobility

The MOVED autonomous hospital bed represents a breakthrough in healthcare technology, combining intelligent mobility with patient safety and operational efficiency.

Key Features

🛡️ Advanced Fall Prevention

Multi-layered safety system with automatic stabilization, side guard rails, and emergency lockdown features. Prevents patient falls during autonomous movement and positioning changes.

🤖 Autonomous Navigation

Self-driving capability with advanced sensor technology allows the bed to navigate hospital corridors safely and efficiently, reducing manual handling and transportation time.

🛠️ Easy Maintenance

Modular design with easy-to-replace components. Regular self-diagnostics alert staff to maintenance needs, reducing unexpected failures.

📋 EHR Integration

Direct integration with Electronic Health Records systems automatically logs patient movements, positioning changes, and vital sign data for comprehensive medical records.

🚨 Emergency Response Mode

Rapid response protocols with instant staff alerting system. Emergency override capabilities ensure immediate manual control when needed for critical situations.

🔒 Enhanced Safety

Collision detection, emergency stop functions, and safety rails ensure patient security during movement and rest. Automatic braking and stability systems.

🦠 Infection Control Technology

Antimicrobial surfaces and UV-C sanitization system eliminate harmful pathogens. Self-cleaning capability reduces cross-contamination between patients.

📱 IoT Integration

Connected device ecosystem with real-time data monitoring, remote control capabilities, and integration with hospital management systems.

⚡ Long Battery Life

Extended battery capacity enables continuous operation throughout shifts with minimal downtime. Fast-charging capability for quick turnarounds.

🏥 Nurses Station Integration

Seamless integration with hospital nurses stations enables autonomous movement of patients between rooms and floors. Built-in elevator compatibility allows automatic multi-floor navigation.

💪 Pressure Ulcer Prevention

Dynamic weight distribution technology and automated repositioning protocols reduce pressure ulcer risk. Smart cushioning adapts to individual patient needs.

📊 Real-Time Patient Monitoring

Integrated vital signs monitoring system tracks patient health metrics during transport. Automatic alert system notifies medical staff of critical changes in real-time.

📊 Smart Positioning

Motorized adjustable height, backrest, and leg sections for optimal patient comfort and clinical positioning. Customizable presets for different care scenarios.

🗣️ Voice Control & AI Assistant

Natural language voice commands allow patients and staff to control bed positioning, request movement, and communicate with the system hands-free.
